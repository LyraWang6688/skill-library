# WeChat Publish Checklist

## Final decision levels

- 可发布
- 建议修改后发布
- 暂不建议发布

## Checklist

Check:

1. Title is clear, specific, searchable, and not title-bait.
2. Opening directly enters the problem.
3. Article focuses on one core question.
4. Reader identity and scenario are clear.
5. Real experience is present.
6. Concrete examples or project details are present.
7. Logic is smooth and headings are self-explanatory.
8. There are shareable and citable summary sentences.
9. Tone is sincere, restrained, and trustworthy.
10. AI effects are not exaggerated.
11. Paragraphs are short enough for mobile reading.
12. GEO summary exists and includes audience, problem, scenario, and experience.
13. Technical and factual claims have been checked.
14. Political/economic/financial claims have authoritative sources if present.
15. HTML/CSS layout uses the official brand color system.
16. Cover concept matches the article topic and brand style.
17. Correct column ending image is inserted or placeholder is clearly marked.
18. Missing assets are listed clearly.

## Publication material completeness

Confirm whether the following exist:

- final title
- polished article body
- 120-character summary
- keywords
- long-tail search questions
- fact-check report or risk notes
- HTML/CSS layout
- cover prompt
- column ending image or placeholder
