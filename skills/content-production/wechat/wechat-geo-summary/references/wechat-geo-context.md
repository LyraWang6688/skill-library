# WeChat GEO Context

## Goal

Make articles easier for AI search systems and readers to understand, summarize, cite, and retrieve.

The account is no longer limited to AI Coding product implementation. It now focuses on non-technical AI collaboration practice: real scenarios, concrete value, implementable solutions, tool combination, product/system building, workflow integration, and knowledge infrastructure.

## Summary must include

1. 明确人群：非技术背景、业务人员、产品经理、运营人员、普通人、AI 初学者、创业者等。
2. 明确问题：如何与 AI 协作、如何拆解问题、如何组合工具、如何做小产品 / 小系统、API 怎么理解、MCP 是什么等。
3. 明确场景：真实工作场景、产品落地、工作流、工具组合、知识系统、React、提示词、企业场景等。
4. 有经验感：体现真实实践、真实案例、第一手经验、卡点和解决方案。
5. 有价值判断：说明 AI 在该场景中的价值，而不是只说工具功能。

## Useful formula

很多人以为____。但我真正实践后发现____。这篇文章会分享____。

Alternative formula:

AI 是很强大的工具，但在____场景里，真正重要的是____。这篇文章会结合我的真实实践，分享____。

## Column-specific GEO notes

### AI 协作实验

Summaries should include the specific project, blocker, tool combination, concept, or workflow being discussed.

### 边走边想

Summaries should include the concrete life/work/career situation and the thinking shift.

### 书籍推荐

Summaries should include the book, the core concept, and how it changed understanding.

### 热钱之外

Summaries should include investor identity, market/risk scenario, and rational judgment point. Avoid investment promises.

### AI 简报

Summaries should identify the time-sensitive AI development, affected audience, source type, trend/opportunity/boundary, and labor market impact when relevant.

## Avoid

- 空泛口号。
- 只写“本文介绍了……”这类弱摘要。
- 只写情绪，不写问题和场景。
- 堆砌关键词。
- 只强调 AI 很强，但不说明场景、价值或解决方案。
